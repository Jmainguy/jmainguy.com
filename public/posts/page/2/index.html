Bacon Wrapped, Old Bay Seasoned, Ribeye Burgers, dressed with East Carolina Vinegar based BBQ sauce

Leah said it was burger night, so might as well do it right. Bought a ribeye steak, then sliced it up and cut off the excess fat and tissue (leave some of the fat, otherwise you get a dry burger). Formed patties, seasoned it with salt and old bay. Wrap it with bacon. Place on grill. Once its nearly done, grill your buns and top burgers with cheese. Leave burgers in until the cheese is molten, and dripping into the fire.

Cooked some Barbacoa

Made some Barbacoa for burrito’s tonight, really turned out great.
