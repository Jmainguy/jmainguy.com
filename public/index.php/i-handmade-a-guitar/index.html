I handmade a guitar

December 5, 2016 One-minute read
General

Finally wrapped up my big guitar project. Finished the guitar yesterday and learned how to play it correctly today (have to use a slide or it wont sound right). Pretty happy with how it turned out.

Metal rod for nut. Straight cut headstock. Wood burned fret markers made by hand. Metal rod again for the bridge. Will clean up these sound holes later this week. Piezo jack pickup.
