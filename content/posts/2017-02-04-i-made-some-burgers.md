Cooked the burger 3 minutes on each side and it came out medium, I prefer medium rare so will cook a little less next time. The meat was pretty hard to cut with my dull knives, will try grinding in food processor next time. All in all it was great. I also made Leah a bacon and egg sammy but didn&#8217;t have time to take any pics of that, baby mainguy was hungry. I can now be called two meal Johnny.
